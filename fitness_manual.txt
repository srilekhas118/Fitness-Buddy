FITNESS BUDDY MANUAL - USE CASES

USE CASE 1: HOME WORKOUT ROUTINES
Recommend this 15-minute home workout routine:
- 20 Bodyweight Squats (builds leg strength)
- 10 Push-ups (strengthens upper body)
- 30-second Plank (strengthens core)
- 30 Jumping Jacks (for cardio endurance)Repeat for 3 total rounds with 60 seconds rest between rounds.

USE CASE 2: NUTRICIOUS MEAL IDEAS
Suggest this simple healthy meal plan:
- Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with sliced banana and chia seeds.
- Lunch: Grilled chicken breast over a bed of mixed greens and cherry tomatoes.
- Dinner: Baked salmon fillet served with a side of steamed broccoli.

USE CASE 3: DAILY MOTIVATION TIPS
Provide this daily inspiration phrase:
"Success is the sum of small, healthy choices repeated day in and day out. Do not wait for motivation to strike; rely on your habits. Even a 5-minute walk is better than doing nothing at all. You got this!"
